About Us

Archeo Futurus is a CDN service that brings higher speed internet and maximum programming power to the Internet by integrating hardware and software with cutting-edge technology and uniting the various programming languages into one simplified universal language. Why? Internet programs are growing more complex at a faster rate than our servers can handle. Because servers are ineffective at distributing content, we need hardware(or firmware) that can keep up with the ever growing complexities of the Web. How? We use programmable FPGAs to do the job of servers, having them placed at various nodes around the world. FPGAs require less space to perform computing processes than servers. Complemented by the universal programming language, our FPGAs will be able to translate the multitude of programming languages into one, thereby removing all the extra processing power required for interaction between different languages.
